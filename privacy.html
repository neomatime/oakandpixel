Oak & Pixel (Pty) Ltd (“Oak & Pixel”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting personal information in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act, 2013 (“POPIA”).

1. Purpose

This policy explains how we collect, use, store, protect, and disclose personal information when you interact with our website, services, or communications.

2. Information we collect

  • Full name and business/company details
  • Contact information (email address, phone number)
  • Billing details (where applicable)
  • Website usage data (cookies, analytics)
  • Information you voluntarily provide to us

3. How we use personal information

  • To provide services and respond to enquiries
  • To communicate with clients and prospects
  • For billing and administrative purposes
  • To improve website performance and user experience
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Legal basis for processing

We process personal information where:

  • you have provided consent;
  • processing is necessary to perform a contract with you;
  • processing is required by law; or
  • processing is necessary for legitimate business interests.

5. Disclosure and sharing

We may share personal information with trusted third parties such as hosting providers, payment processors, professional advisors, and regulators where legally required. We do not sell personal information.

6. Security

We apply appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal information against unauthorised access, loss, misuse, or alteration.

7. Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law.

8. Your rights under POPIA

  • Request access to your personal information
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Object to processing
  • Withdraw consent (where applicable)
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator (South Africa)

9. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve user experience and analyse traffic. See our Cookie Policy for details.

10. Cross-border transfers

Where personal information is transferred outside South Africa, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place in accordance with POPIA.
