This Cookie Policy explains how Oak & Pixel uses cookies and similar technologies on our website. It should be read alongside our Privacy Policy.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help a website function, improve user experience, and provide analytical insights.

2. Why we use cookies

  • To ensure core website functionality
  • To improve performance and user experience
  • To analyse traffic and usage patterns
  • To support security and fraud prevention
  • To support marketing effectiveness (where used)

3. Types of cookies we use

3.1 Essential cookies

Required for the website to function and cannot be disabled through our systems.

3.2 Performance & analytics cookies

Help us understand usage (pages visited, time on site) to improve the website.

3.3 Functional cookies

Remember preferences and improve the experience.

3.4 Marketing cookies

May be used to measure campaigns or show relevant content (only where enabled).

4. Third-party cookies

Some trusted service providers may set cookies (e.g., analytics). We recommend reviewing their privacy policies for more information.

5. Managing cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect site functionality.

6. Legal basis

In line with POPIA, we process personal information collected via cookies lawfully and transparently. Where required, we obtain consent for non-essential cookies.

7. Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. The latest version will be published on our website.
